I started my Ph.D at University of Southern California from Fall 2011. I worked with Professor Cyrus Shahabi in several projects, iCampus, iWatch and MediaQ. My research area is spatial crowdsourcing. Besides, I completed two summer interns (2012, 2014) at Teradata, Query Optimizer Group.

Before joining USC, I worked at R&D Lab of VNG Corporation, an on-line gaming and social network company with 8M active users. We developed a feed ranking system on the social network and several backends for social games. At VNG, I studied faceted search and social search, and developed a real-time people search engine for our social network, named ZingMe.

I obtained my BS of Engineering in Computer Science from Hanoi University of Science and Technology in June 2010. During my undergraduate studies, I worked for a mobile company (VMG) for two years, then joined a High Performance Computing Center to do my thesis on a topic namely, grid-based system for document plagiarism detection.
